About Us

Welcome to AVANTIKA.

AVANTIKA Salon was founded in 2007 at a prime location in Vadodara with the aim of providing a luxurious salon experience to clients. Ever since then, we have created a reputation for ourselves for being the best salon in the city for all things hair, beauty, makeup, and wellness. We provide exceptional quality services to our clients, having a staff that has been trained by national and international level trainers, enabling us to be one of the best unisex beauty salons in Vadodara.
